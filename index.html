Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Find out everything you need to know about ordering custom trophies and medals

What types of customization do you offer?

We offer comprehensive customization including name engraving, date engraving, logo placement, custom text messages, and various finish options (gold, silver, bronze). You can also choose from multiple sizes for most products.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Our standard turnaround time is 3-5 business days for most orders. Rush orders can be accommodated for an additional fee, with same-day or next-day options available for select items and quantities.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer competitive volume pricing for bulk orders. The more you order, the better the per-unit price. Contact us with your quantity requirements for a custom quote tailored to your budget.

What areas do you deliver to?

We offer delivery throughout the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex including Dallas, Fort Worth, Arlington, Plano, Irving, Garland, Frisco, McKinney, and surrounding areas. Pickup is also available at our DFW location.

Can I see a proof before production?

Absolutely! We provide digital proofs for all custom engraving orders via WhatsApp or email. We'll make sure everything looks perfect before we begin production on your trophies or medals.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, Zelle, Venmo, and business checks. Payment terms can be discussed for large corporate orders.
